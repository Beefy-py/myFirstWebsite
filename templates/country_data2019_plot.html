This is a Bokeh Plot i made using a bit of data i scraped from Wikipedia
It is the population of 229 countries of 2019.

As you hover the cursor in the area of the countries, you can see a bit more info about them.
The bars of the countries with the least population are a so thin so you might want to zoom in a lot and then hover over it to see the info.
There are tools to the right of the plot, the zoom tool is one of them
